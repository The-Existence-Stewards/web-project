How does it work?

Let's see together how you can Level up!

Step 1

On your profile page, you can see all the categories that you can use to track your activities. After doing some Strength activity, or something that goes into the Intelligence category, enter your minutes with the "Add progress" button.

Step 2

When you have entered your minutes, you can see the Level up-bar gets fuller and fuller. Enter more minutes to go to the next level!

Step 3

See on your profile card your Level, and check what is your highest score. Tip: to get a higher score, you can try new activities. Enjoy!
